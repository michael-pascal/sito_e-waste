Work exploitation

In addition to the many problems it brought to the environment, E-waste also brought many
issues to the world of work,many people that struggle to find a job work end up
in E-waste recycling centers in which they are usually exploited. This situation happens
in both developing and developed countries.

These workers normally come from poorer countries where they are pretty much forced
to work, otherwise they wouldn’t have any money to buy food so their only option is to work
under these E-waste centers even though they know they are being exploited.

moreover these recycling centers make the workers under them work in extremely harsh
conditions in which they labour with very toxic substances besides getting underpaid,
sometimes what they earn isn’t enough for their basic necessities so even children
have to work.

The objective of these E-waste centers is to take E-waste,make the workers extract the
materials and resell them for a profit,an example of countries where this happened or still
happens is in China and Palestine.
