Introduction

E-waste is a problem we all have to face ,but what is it? E-waste refers to those devices,
such as computers,phones or home appliances that have been discarded by people,
whether they work or not.This trash is really dangerous because of some toxic
chemicals they release when buried underground.

Since the last few decades with the evolution of technology there has been an extremely fast
increase of new devices and with it electronic waste, this affected both developed and
developing countries,in 2014-2016 there has been an increase of 8% of E-waste in only 2
years with millions of metric tonnes,but only a small 20% of it was recycled.

So where does so much garbage end up? For many years it ended up in landfills where they
